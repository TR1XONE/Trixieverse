## Key Refinements Made

### 1. **Community Building (Discord) - Made Realistic**

**Before:** "1,000 members by launch"  
**After:** Phased growth with clear milestones
- Week 1-2: 500 members (friends & testers)
- Week 3-4: 1,500 members (pre-launch momentum)
- Launch: 5,000+ members
- Month 1: 15,000+ members

**Why:** 1,000 is arbitrary. This shows realistic velocity.

**Additional improvements:**
- Added specific automation (welcome bot, reaction roles, daily messages)
- Clarified moderation structure (3-4 mods minimum for 24/7 coverage)
- Added channel purposes (many apps fail from poor channel design)

---

### 2. **Reddit Strategy - From Vague to Specific**

**Before:** "Weekly AMA and player stories"  
**After:** 4-week structured content calendar with engagement tactics

**Week 1:** Foundation-building posts with active commenting  
**Week 2:** Feature deep-dives (technical + visual)  
**Week 3:** Hype building (testimonials + preview)  
**Week 4:** Launch week execution (megathread + day-1 success)

**Engagement metrics:** Target 50k impressions, 10% CTR, 100+ signups

**Why:** Vague posting = vague results. This gives daily direction.

---

### 3. **Twitter Strategy - Structured Framework**

**Before:** "10k followers by launch" (unrealistic for unknown product)  
**After:** Realistic growth + content pillars approach

**Growth targets:**
- Week 1: 500 followers
- Week 4: 5,000 followers
- Month 1: 15,000 followers
- Month 3: 50,000+ followers

**Content breakdown:**
- 35% Coach personality (differentiation)
- 25% Education (value)
- 25% Community (engagement)
- 15% Polls/engagement (feedback)

**Why:** Specific content pillars = consistency = algorithm rewards

---

### 4. **Influencer Outreach - Made Realistic & Actionable**

**Before:** Vague "reach out to influencers"  
**After:** Three-tier approach with realistic response rates

**Tier 1 (50k+ followers):** 5-10 creators, 20-30% response, personalized emails  
**Tier 2 (10k-50k followers):** 20-30 creators, 40-50% response, personalized DMs  
**Tier 3 (1k-10k followers):** 50-100 creators, 60%+ response, mass personalization

**Key improvement:** Added influencer selection criteria (go/no-go checklist)
- Recent Wild Rift content
- Engagement rate >3%
- Non-toxic community
- Consistent posting

**Why:** Micro-influencers have better ROI + more authentic partnerships

### 5. **Content Creation - Made Practical**

**After:** Tiered approach showing minimum viable content

**Tier 1 (MUST HAVE):**

**Tier 2 (SHOULD HAVE):**

- This product will grow month-over-month, not hockey-stick

- Plan for 20-30% monthly growth initially

### Community is Your Product
- Marketing isn't separate from product

- Phone recordings + Canva graphics > professional editors
- Authentic beats polished

- "500 ‚Üí 5,000 ‚Üí 15,000" is achievable
